Adoption Guidelines

At PetConnect, we ensure that every adoption is handled with care, transparency, and proper legal verification. The following guidelines explain how the adoption process works for rescued and found pets.

  • 🐕‍🦺 If any pet is found by a user and remains without an owner for 15 days, the pet becomes eligible for adoption.
  • 🐾 Such pets are verified and listed by PetConnect as ready for adoption.
  • 📞 When a user makes an adoption request, they receive a verification call from PetConnect to confirm their details and intent.
  • 📋 After the necessary legal procedures and documentation, the pet is officially handed over to its new owner.
  • ❤️ PetConnect ensures every pet is adopted into a safe, loving, and responsible home.

Thank you for choosing to adopt, not shop. Every adoption helps give an animal a second chance at happiness.
