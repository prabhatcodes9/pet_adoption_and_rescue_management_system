About Us

Welcome to PetConnect — your trusted online platform for connecting pets with loving families across the nation.

Our mission is to build a compassionate community where every pet finds a home. Whether you have lost your pet, found someone else’s pet, or wish to adopt an abandoned animal, PetConnect is here to help.

🐶 Lost a pet? — Report it on our platform so nearby users can help locate your furry friend.
🐕‍🦺 Found a pet? — Upload the pet’s details and help us reunite them with their owner.
🐾 Want to adopt? — Explore the list of abandoned or rescued pets ready for adoption.

Every small action makes a big difference in saving lives and spreading kindness. Join PetConnect and be a part of our mission to bring pets and people together with love and care.
